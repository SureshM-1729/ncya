Scholarships

There are many scholarships available to help you fund your education. Find the best options for your field of study.

NSP - National Scholarship Portal

NSP offers a range of scholarships for students across various levels of education, including pre-matric and post-matric scholarships.

Explore NSP Scholarships

ePASS - Electronic Payment and Application System of Scholarships

ePASS is an online system for disbursing scholarships to students in various states, helping streamline the scholarship application process.

Explore ePASS Scholarships

NMMS - National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship

NMMS offers scholarships to students from economically weaker sections to continue their education at the secondary stage.

Apply for NMMS Scholarship
